Dataset GTZAN — music genre classification
Source kaggle.com/andradaolteanu/gtzan-dataset-music-genre-classification
Composition 1,000 audio clips · 30 s each · 10 genres × 100 (blues, classical, country, disco, hiphop, jazz, metal, pop, reggae, rock)
Topology genres_original (30 s WAVs) · images_original (color spectrograms) · features_30_sec.csv + features_3_sec.csv (pre-computed audio features)
Completeness ~99% complete; rows with empty/unusable features are skipped. Aligning spectrograms with the feature CSVs leaves a ~10% gap, so validation sets are held to ≥90% coverage.
Model B.E.A.R. D.O.W.N. — Bayesian Evaluation of Audio Representations: A Deep Optimized Waveform Network
Source: INFO 510 Fall 2025 final project report (Group 7).
