PHONON

phonon density-of-states reproduction

Project Phonon reproduces Chen et al. (2021): an E(3)-equivariant graph network that predicts a crystal's phonon density of states — the spectrum of its lattice vibrations — from atomic structure alone. mk1 is the faithful deterministic baseline, verified against the authors' own checkpoint. mk2 wraps it in a Bayesian last-layer head for calibrated uncertainty; mk3 adds information-theory metrics and architecture sweeps.
